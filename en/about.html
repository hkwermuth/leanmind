About LeanMind

Behind LeanMind is me – Hannah Kamstrup Wermuth. I hold an MSc in physical geography and computer science from the University of Copenhagen (2000) with further training in lean, coaching, project management, organisation & leadership.

I am passionate about creating sustainable change through optimisation, efficiency and developing people. With solid experience in Lean, process improvement and leadership coaching I have over the years worked to identify improvement potential and turn it into concrete, measurable results.

I also work actively with modern AI tools to strengthen analysis, structuring and development of solutions. That allows me to quickly create overview, test ideas and turn complex needs into concrete concepts – such as the development of this website, built in collaboration with AI.

I build on strong results from organisations including Novo Nordisk, DSB, DTU and Sund & Bælt, where I have delivered measurable improvements in complex operational environments.

I am based in the Copenhagen area but take on assignments across the Kingdom of Denmark.
