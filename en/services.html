My Services

I help both small and large organisations create more value by optimising processes through the Lean mindset. The aim is to improve efficiency and reduce waste, leading to stronger results and more satisfied customers. This can include:

  • Strategy / Policy Deployment: To ensure alignment through the organisation with the company strategy and goals
  • Waste analysis: Based on go-look-see and data review to identify improvement potential
  • Value stream analysis: To map where waste occurs so we can increase value creation in your processes
  • Leadership coaching: I help develop the leaders who will drive change through coaching
  • Implementation of lean tools, e.g. performance boards, problem solving, continuous improvement
  • Standardisation of tasks/processes or revision of existing procedures
  • Optimal workplace organisation through 5S
  • Facilitation of complex problem solving
  • Employee training in lean and problem solving
